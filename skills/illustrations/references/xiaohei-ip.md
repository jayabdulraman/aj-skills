# Xiaohei IP

## Character Definition

Xiaohei is Ian's recurring visual IP character for article illustrations.

Xiaohei should appear by default in every illustration. Xiaohei is not a mascot, sticker, or cute decoration. Xiaohei is an absurd worker seriously participating in the operation of the system.

## Appearance

- Small solid-black creature.
- White dot eyes.
- Thin legs, sometimes thin arms.
- Body can resemble a cylinder, black bean, black box, funnel, shadow, hole, or dark block inside a machine.
- Slightly irregular hand-drawn outline.
- Blank, calm, deadpan, serious expression.

## Personality

- Very serious, while doing something slightly absurd.
- Like a low-profile system operator.
- Dry humor, not cute.
- A little clumsy, but not stupid.
- Feels genuinely responsible for a task inside a whiteboard sketch.

## Common Jobs

Make Xiaohei perform the core action:

- Carrying raw material.
- Pulling lines to gather information sources.
- Getting stuck in a breakpoint.
- Operating a judgment lever inside a machine.
- Becoming a filtering funnel.
- Cutting a source-fish into reusable pieces.
- Stamping handoff copy.
- Pulling a handoff path into place.
- Holding a warning sign near a pitfall path.
- Reaching from a hole and failing to catch content.
- Building, bridging, opening doors, sorting, recording, repairing, or routing.

## Avoid

- Do not make Xiaohei an overly cute mascot.
- Do not make Xiaohei a children's cartoon character.
- Do not add complex clothes, meme expressions, or shiny eyes.
- Do not leave Xiaohei standing in a corner watching.
- Do not let Xiaohei overpower the structure being explained.
- Do not make Xiaohei too commercial, too round, or too polished.

## Evaluation

If the core metaphor still works perfectly after removing Xiaohei, Xiaohei is too decorative. Rewrite the prompt so Xiaohei becomes the subject of the action.
