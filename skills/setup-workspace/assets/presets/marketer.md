# Preset: Marketer

## When to use
Role is marketing manager, content/growth/campaign marketer, comms. Runs campaigns, produces content/copy, manages channels, tracks performance.

## Suggested structure
```
campaigns/                # one folder per campaign
  <campaign>/
    brief/  creative/ (copy, assets)  channels/ (email, social, web, paid)  calendar/  results/
content/                  # evergreen content library, messaging, brand voice
templates/  work-memory/  connectors/  resources/  archive/
```

## Recommended templates
campaign-brief · content-calendar · messaging-framework · email-copy · social-post · launch-comms · performance-report

## AGENTS.md role snippet
> Focus areas: campaign strategy, content production, channel management, performance. Delegate: drafting copy, building calendars, repurposing content across channels, performance summaries. Apply brand voice and visual guidelines by default; keep claims accurate and compliant.

## Tailoring notes
Ask their channels, CMS/email platform, analytics, and brand guidelines. Bake the messaging framework and voice into `content/`. If branded visuals are involved, pair with the org's design-system skill.
