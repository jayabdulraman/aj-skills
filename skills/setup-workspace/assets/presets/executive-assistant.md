# Preset: Executive Assistant

## When to use
Role is EA, executive/admin assistant, chief of staff (light). Manages calendars, travel, meetings, communications, and recurring operational duties for one or more executives.

## Suggested structure
```
execs/                    # one folder per person supported
  <exec>/  briefings/  meetings/  travel/  expenses/  correspondence/
operations/               # recurring duties, SOPs, vendor/contacts
calendar/                 # planning, agendas, prep docs
templates/  work-memory/  connectors/  resources/  archive/
```
(Time/cadence is the natural organizing axis here — group by recurring duty and by person supported.)

## Recommended templates
meeting-agenda · meeting-briefing · travel-itinerary · weekly-priorities · email-draft · sop

## AGENTS.md role snippet
> Focus areas: calendar/inbox management, meeting prep, travel, executive comms, keeping things running. Delegate: drafting briefings and agendas, formatting itineraries, prepping meeting docs, summarizing inboxes. Handle scheduling/contact details discreetly; confirm before sending anything on the exec's behalf.

## Tailoring notes
Ask which execs they support, calendar/email/travel/expense tools, and standing meetings. Build briefing + weekly-priorities templates first — those get the most reuse. Respect confidentiality around schedules and contacts.
