# Preset: Success Coach

## When to use
Role is student/learner success coach, academic advisor, retention coach. Supports a caseload of students/learners; tracks outreach, progress, interventions; runs cohort programming.

## Suggested structure
```
caseload/                 # organized by cohort or term (avoid storing sensitive student data here when possible)
  <cohort>/  outreach-logs/  at-risk/  notes/
programming/              # workshops, resources, cadences, playbooks
reporting/                # caseload metrics, outcomes
templates/  work-memory/  connectors/  resources/  archive/
```

## Recommended templates
outreach-message · coaching-session-notes · intervention-plan · check-in-cadence · resource-guide · caseload-report

## AGENTS.md role snippet
> Focus areas: proactive outreach, coaching conversations, removing barriers, retention. Delegate: drafting outreach, formatting session notes, building cadences and resource guides, summarizing caseload trends. **Student data is sensitive (FERPA)** — keep identifiable info out of shared/synced outputs; prefer IDs over names in anything that leaves the workspace.

## Tailoring notes
Ask their SIS/CRM (the system of record for student data — keep it there), comms channels, and any retention playbook. Default to privacy: this workspace holds playbooks, templates, and de-identified summaries, not the student record.
