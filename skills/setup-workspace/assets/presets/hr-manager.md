# Preset: HR Manager / Assistant

## When to use
Role is HR manager/assistant/business partner, people ops, talent/recruiting coordinator. Handles hiring, onboarding, policy, employee relations, HR comms.

## Suggested structure
```
functions/
  recruiting/ (reqs, JDs, pipelines, interview kits)
  onboarding/ (checklists, plans)
  policies/   (handbook, SOPs)
  employee-relations/ (CONFIDENTIAL — sensitive cases)
  comms/      (announcements, FAQs)
templates/  work-memory/  connectors/  resources/  archive/
```

## Recommended templates
job-description · interview-kit · onboarding-checklist · policy-sop · hr-announcement · offer-summary

## AGENTS.md role snippet
> Focus areas: hiring, onboarding, policy, people support, HR comms. Delegate: drafting JDs/policies/announcements, building checklists and interview kits, formatting docs. **Confidentiality is paramount** — never expose personal/employee data in shared outputs; keep `employee-relations/` private and treat its contents as sensitive.

## Tailoring notes
Ask their HRIS/ATS, doc home, and comms channel. Emphasize confidentiality and access boundaries; clearly mark sensitive folders. Keep policy content aligned with the official handbook as source of truth.
