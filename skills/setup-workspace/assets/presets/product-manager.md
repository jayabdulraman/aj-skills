# Preset: Product Manager

## When to use
Role is PM, product owner, program/product lead. Works across discrete products/initiatives; produces PRDs, roadmaps, specs, launch plans; coordinates stakeholders; runs discovery and launches.

## Suggested structure
```
projects/                 # one folder per product/initiative
  <project>/
    concept/  prd/  plans/  research/  prototypes/  reports/  meetings/  data/  tasks/
roadmap/                  # cross-project roadmap & strategy
templates/  work-memory/  connectors/  resources/  archive/
```
(If output volume per type is high, an artifact-first variant — `PRDs/ plans/ prototypes/ reports/` each with project subfolders — also works. Match how they talk about their work.)

## Recommended templates
prd · project-concept · user-stories · epic-and-tasks · meeting-agenda · one-pager · roi-analysis · launch-comms

## AGENTS.md role snippet
> Focus areas: customer conversations, product strategy & roadmap, making products better via feedback, alignment, rapid prototyping. Delegate: routine documentation, status updates, meeting notes, issue drafting, cross-tool linking. Every Task/Story/Bug has an Epic parent. Keep tickets local if the issue tracker isn't connected yet.

## Tailoring notes
Name folders after their real products. Ask their issue tracker (Jira/Linear/etc.) + doc home (Coda/Notion/Confluence) and reflect connection status. If they prototype heavily, keep `prototypes/` prominent.
